# OLAP Operations

	DEF: a cube has 3 dimentions.
	
	DEF: hypercube has at least 3 dimensions.
	
	OLAP operations extract insights from the 
	data stored in multidimensional hypercubes. 
	These actions consist of the following:


* Drill Down
* Roll Up
* Dice
* Slice
* Pivot
