# Week 1 Lab — Student Version (Detailed)

## 🎯 Objective
Learn how to translate business questions into SQL queries and interpret results.

---

## 🧠 Key Concept
You are NOT writing SQL.
You are answering business questions using data.

---

## Part 1 — Exploration

### Task 1
Show first 10 rows.

👉 Think:
- What columns exist?
- What types of data do you see?

---

### Task 2
Select only: age, region, charges

👉 Why?
Focus on relevant columns.

---

## Part 2 — Filtering

### Task 3
Find all smokers.

👉 Business question:
Who are high-risk customers?

---

### Task 4
Find customers with charges > 15000

👉 Business meaning:
Who are expensive customers?

---

## Part 3 — Aggregation

### Task 5
Average charges per region

👉 Question:
Which region is most expensive?

---

### Task 6
Average charges per smoker

👉 Question:
Do smokers pay more?

---

### Task 7
Count customers per region

👉 Question:
Where do most customers live?

---

## Part 4 — Multi-Dimensional Analysis

### Task 8
Average charges by region AND smoker

👉 Question:
Which segment is most expensive?

---

## Part 5 — Challenge

### Task 9
Find high-risk customers:
- smoker = yes
- bmi > 30

👉 Question:
Who are the most dangerous segment?

---

## 🧠 Reflection

- What variable impacts cost most?
- What surprised you?
- What would you recommend to the business?
