
# 🚴 Bike Store <br> 10 Insightful OLAP Queries

Based on a Bike Store Star Schema:

- **Fact Table**: `sales`
- **Dimension Tables**: `stores`, `products`, `customers`, `dates`

These OLAP queries are written in business-friendly English and can be translated into SQL for implementation.

---

## 1️⃣ Total Sales by Year

**Business Question:**  
What is the total revenue generated each year?

**Insight:**  
Identifies long-term growth trends and year-over-year performance.

---

## 2️⃣ Monthly Sales Trend (Time-Series Analysis)

**Business Question:**  
What are total monthly sales across all stores?

**Insight:**  
Helps detect seasonality (e.g., summer bike demand peaks).

---

## 3️⃣ Top 10 Best-Selling Products

**Business Question:**  
What are the top 10 products ranked by total sales revenue?

**Insight:**  
Identifies high-performing inventory items.

---

## 4️⃣ Sales by Product Category

**Business Question:**  
What is total revenue generated by each product category (e.g., Mountain, Road, Electric)?

**Insight:**  
Reveals which categories drive overall profitability.

---

## 5️⃣ Store Performance Comparison

**Business Question:**  
Which stores generate the highest total revenue?

**Insight:**  
Compares store productivity and regional performance.

---

## 6️⃣ Sales by Country (Customer Geography)

**Business Question:**  
What is total revenue grouped by customer country?

**Insight:**  
Identifies strongest geographic markets.

---

## 7️⃣ Average Order Value by Store

**Business Question:**  
What is the average sales amount per transaction for each store?

**Insight:**  
Measures customer spending behavior across locations.

---

## 8️⃣ Customer Gender-Based Sales Analysis

**Business Question:**  
What percentage of total sales comes from male vs female customers?

**Insight:**  
Supports targeted marketing strategies.

---

## 9️⃣ Quarterly Category Performance (Roll-Up Analysis)

**Business Question:**  
For each year and quarter, what is total revenue per product category?

**Insight:**  
Combines time roll-up (Year → Quarter) with product segmentation.

---

## 🔟 Top 5 Customers by Lifetime Value

**Business Question:**  
Who are the top 5 customers ranked by total lifetime purchase amount?

**Insight:**  
Identifies high-value customers for loyalty programs.

---
